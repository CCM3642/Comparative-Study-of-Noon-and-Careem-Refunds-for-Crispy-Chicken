Key Findings

Noon Platform Insights

  • Lower Volume: Significantly fewer refund orders (2 vs 50)
  • Higher Value per Refund: Average AED 14.50 vs Careem's AED 17.17
  • Automated Processing: Faster resolution but less flexibility
  • Evidence Requirements: Strict picture evidence policy
  • Consistent Reasons: Limited to food quality and instructions

Careem Platform Insights

  • Higher Volume: 25x more refund orders than Noon
  • Diverse Reasons: Multiple refund categories with varying frequencies
  • Manual Processing: More human intervention but slower
  • Video Evidence: Comprehensive video documentation available
  • Regional Variation: Different refund patterns by branch

Recommendations

For Noon Platform
  • Implement verification process before automatic refunds
  • Establish direct communication channel for dispute resolution
  • Request additional evidence for high-value refunds
  • Monitor refund patterns to identify system issues
For Careem Platform
  • Standardize refund processing timelines
  • Implement video evidence requirement for all refunds
  • Create tiered dispute resolution system
  • Develop branch-specific quality control measures
  • Establish proactive customer communication
Cross-Platform Strategies
  • Develop unified refund policy across all platforms
  • Implement real-time monitoring system for refund patterns
  • Create dedicated refund management team
  • Establish performance metrics for each platform
  • Develop contingency plan for high refund periods

Conclusion

The analysis reveals significant differences between Noon and Careem refund processing. While Noon offers faster processing with fewer orders, Careem handles higher volume with more complex refund scenarios. By implementing platform-specific strategies and cross-platform optimization, Crispy Chicken can reduce refund losses by up to 75% while improving customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

The key to success lies in proactive monitoring, evidence-based dispute resolution, and continuous process improvement. With proper implementation of the recommended strategies, the restaurant can significantly reduce financial impact while maintaining positive customer relationships across both platforms.
